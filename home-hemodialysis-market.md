<p><ul><li>Homecare Peritoneal Dialysis Machine</li><li>Homecare Hemodialysis Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home hemodialysis market encompasses two primary types: homecare peritoneal dialysis machines and homecare hemodialysis machines. </p><p>Homecare peritoneal dialysis machines facilitate kidney function by using the lining of the abdomen to filter waste from the blood, allowing patients to perform dialysis at home. </p><p>In contrast, homecare hemodialysis machines directly filter blood through an artificial kidney (dialyzer) at home. Both types offer increased convenience, flexibility, and patient autonomy, improving quality of life for individuals with kidney failure.</p></p>
