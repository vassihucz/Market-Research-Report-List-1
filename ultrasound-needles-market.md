<p><p>The ultrasound needles market encompasses various types, primarily including aspiration needles (EUS-FNA) and biopsy needles (EUS-FNB). EUS-FNA needles are designed for fine needle aspiration, enabling clinicians to extract fluid or tissue samples from lesions for diagnosis. In contrast, EUS-FNB needles are utilized for obtaining larger tissue samples, enhancing diagnostic accuracy for malignancies. Both needle types are integral to endoscopic ultrasound procedures, driving advancements in minimally invasive diagnostics and improving patient outcomes in gastrointestinal and other medical applications.</p></p>
