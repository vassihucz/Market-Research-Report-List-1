<p><p>Centrifugal pumps in the pharmaceutical market primarily include single suction and double suction types. Single suction pumps draw fluid from one side, ideal for applications requiring a compact design and lower flow rates. In contrast, double suction pumps pull fluid from both sides, enhancing flow capacity and efficiency, making them suitable for larger-scale production. Both types ensure reliable fluid transfer, critical for maintaining sterile conditions and accurate dosage in pharmaceutical manufacturing processes. They cater to diverse operational needs while ensuring compliance with industry standards.</p></p>
