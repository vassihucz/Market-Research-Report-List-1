<p><strong>The Power Line Communication System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Narrowband PLC</li><li>Broadband PLC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power Line Communication (PLC) systems are categorized into two main types: Narrowband PLC and Broadband PLC. Narrowband PLC operates over low-frequency bands, suitable for applications like smart metering and home automation, offering lower data rates but high reliability over long distances. In contrast, Broadband PLC utilizes higher frequency bands to provide faster data transmission, enabling services such as internet access and multimedia streaming. While Narrowband focuses on low-bandwidth communication, Broadband addresses the demand for high-speed connectivity over existing power lines.</p></p>
