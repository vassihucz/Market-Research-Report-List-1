<p><p>The knitting machines market is primarily categorized into circular knitting machines and flat bed knitting machines. Circular knitting machines are designed for seamless fabric production, ideal for creating tubular garments like t-shirts and hosiery, thereby enhancing efficiency and reducing fabric wastage. In contrast, flat bed knitting machines are versatile and suitable for making a variety of flat fabrics, enabling complex patterns and textures, commonly used in producing sweaters and other intricate designs. Both types serve distinct manufacturing needs in the textile industry.</p></p>
