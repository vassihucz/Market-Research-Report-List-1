<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Swap Station market consists of two primary types: Sub-box Replacement and Chassis Swap. Sub-box Replacement involves exchanging depleted battery packs for fully charged ones, typically designed for compact vehicles, allowing for quick and convenient energy replenishment. Chassis Swap, on the other hand, replaces the entire vehicle chassis, integrating a new battery and drivetrain, suitable for larger vehicles or specialized applications. Both models aim to enhance EV usability by minimizing downtime and maximizing range.</p></p>
