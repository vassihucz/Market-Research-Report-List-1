<p><ul><li>Chute-Type Color Sorter Machine</li><li>Belt-Type Color Sorter Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The color sorter machine market features two primary types: chute-type and belt-type color sorters. Chute-type color sorters utilize gravity to guide materials through a vertical chute, optimizing separation efficiency for bulk items like grains and beans. In contrast, belt-type color sorters employ a conveyor belt system, allowing for smoother and more controlled movement of products, making them ideal for fragile items such as nuts and dried fruits. Both types enhance product quality by effectively removing defects and impurities.</p></p>
