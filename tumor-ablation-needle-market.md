<p><p>The tumor ablation needle market includes several types of technologies designed to destroy cancer cells. Microwave ablation needles use high-frequency electromagnetic waves to generate heat, effectively killing tumors. Radiofrequency ablation needles employ high-frequency electricity to produce heat and ablate tissue. Cryoablation needles use extreme cold to freeze and destroy cancerous cells. Nanoknife ablation needles apply focused electrical pulses to create pores in cell membranes, allowing for localized destruction of tumors. Each type offers unique methods for targeted cancer treatment.</p></p>
