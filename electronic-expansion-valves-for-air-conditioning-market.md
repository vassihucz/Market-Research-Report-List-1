<p><p>Electronic Expansion Valves (EEVs) are crucial components in air conditioning systems, regulating refrigerant flow for optimal performance. The market is divided into two main types: Electromagnetic EEVs and Electric EEVs. Electromagnetic EEVs use a solenoid mechanism to control valve opening and closing, offering precise flow control. In contrast, Electric EEVs employ electronic actuators for more advanced and responsive adjustments, enhancing system efficiency and temperature management. Both types contribute to improved energy efficiency and comfort in air conditioning applications.</p></p>
