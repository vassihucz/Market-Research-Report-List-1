<p><p>The Radio Frequency (RF) Components market encompasses various essential devices for signal processing in communication systems. Filters eliminate unwanted frequencies, while duplexers enable simultaneous transmission and reception. Power amplifiers boost signal strength, and antenna switches manage signal routing. Modulators encode information onto carrier signals, and demodulators extract the information from received signals. RF switches facilitate signal path management, and low noise amplifiers (LNA) enhance weak signals. Other components may include mixers and oscillators, contributing to a robust RF ecosystem.</p></p>
