<p><p>The hand pump market features various types including the rotary pump, which uses a circular motion to move fluids; the lever action pump, utilizing a lever mechanism for manual operation; and the piston pump, which employs a reciprocating action to generate pressure. Drum siphon pumps facilitate the transfer of liquids from barrels, while hydraulic hand pumps generate high pressure for applications requiring force. Each type serves distinct purposes across industries such as agriculture, construction, and automotive maintenance.</p></p>
