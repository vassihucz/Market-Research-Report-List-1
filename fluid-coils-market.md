<p><p>The fluid coils market comprises two main types: tubed fluid coils and finned fluid coils. Tubed fluid coils utilize a series of tubes for fluid circulation, offering efficient heat exchange for HVAC systems and industrial applications. In contrast, finned fluid coils incorporate fins to increase surface area, enhancing heat transfer efficiency, making them ideal for air conditioning and refrigeration systems. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, emphasizing energy efficiency and performance in thermal management solutions.</p></p>
