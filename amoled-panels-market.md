<p><p>AMOLED panels, or Active Matrix Organic Light Emitting Diode panels, are categorized mainly into flexible and rigid types. Flexible AMOLED panels are lightweight and can be curved or bent, making them ideal for innovative designs in smartphones, wearables, and curved displays. Rigid AMOLED panels, however, offer robustness and superior performance, commonly used in televisions and traditional smartphones. Both types provide vibrant colors, deep blacks, and energy efficiency, catering to diverse consumer preferences and applications in modern electronics.</p></p>
