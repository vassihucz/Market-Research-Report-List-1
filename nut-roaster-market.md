<p><ul><li>Hot-Air Type</li><li>Direct-Fire Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nut roaster market comprises two primary types: hot-air and direct-fire. Hot-air roasters utilize heated air circulation to evenly roast nuts, ensuring consistent results while preserving flavor and nutritional quality. This method is often energy-efficient and reduces the risk of burning. In contrast, direct-fire roasters employ flames to directly roast nuts, providing a distinct flavor and aroma. However, this method requires careful monitoring to prevent overcooking. Both types cater to different preferences in nut roasting techniques and end products.</p></p>
