<p><p>The optical fiber connector market comprises several types, each designed for specific applications and requirements. FC connectors offer low signal loss and are widely used in high-performance situations. SC connectors are known for their easy push-pull coupling, making them popular in data communication. LC connectors are compact and ideal for high-density connections, while ST connectors utilize a bayonet-style coupling for secure connections. Other types include MTP/MPO and E2000 connectors, catering to diverse networking and telecommunications needs.</p></p>
