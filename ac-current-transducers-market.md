<p><p>AC current transducers are essential devices used to measure alternating current in various applications. The market is broadly categorized into two types: single-function and multi-function. Single-function transducers are designed for specific tasks, such as measuring current or voltage, offering simplicity and reliability. In contrast, multi-function transducers can perform several measurements simultaneously, integrating current, voltage, and power analytics in one device. This versatility makes multi-function transducers suitable for complex applications in automation, energy management, and electrical monitoring systems.</p></p>
