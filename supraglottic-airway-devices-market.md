<p><p>The supraglottic airway devices market comprises several types designed for airway management. Laryngeal mask airways (LMAs) offer a supraglottic seal, providing ventilation during anesthesia or emergencies. Oropharyngeal airways facilitate airflow by preventing tongue obstruction in unconscious patients. Nasopharyngeal airways, inserted through the nose, maintain airway patency with comfort. Other supraglottic devices include additional tools like the i-gel or Combitube, catering to varied clinical needs. This market supports emergency medicine, anesthesia, and respiratory care, enhancing patient safety and outcomes.</p></p>
