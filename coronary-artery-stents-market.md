<p><strong>The Coronary Artery Stents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bare-metal Stent (BMS)</li><li>Drug-eluting Stent (DES)</li><li>Bioresorbable Vascular Scaffold (BVS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coronary artery stents market comprises three main types: Bare-metal Stents (BMS), which are simple, stainless steel structures offering immediate vessel support; Drug-eluting Stents (DES), which are coated with medication to prevent restenosis and improve long-term outcomes; and Bioresorbable Vascular Scaffolds (BVS), which provide temporary support to the artery and gradually dissolve, allowing natural vessel function to restore over time. Each type addresses specific patient needs and contributes to advancements in cardiovascular treatment.</p></p>
