<p><p>Event Management Software (EMS) is categorized into two primary markets: On-Premises and Cloud-Based solutions. In contrast, Cloud-Based EMS operates on remote servers, facilitating easy access and collaboration from anywhere with internet connectivity. This model typically involves subscription-based pricing, reducing initial expenses and allowing for seamless updates, making it an increasingly popular choice for organizations of all sizes.</p></p>
