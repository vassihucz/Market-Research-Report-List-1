<p><p>The ozone analyzer market is categorized into three main types: UV photometric, electrochemical, and others. UV photometric analyzers utilize ultraviolet light to detect ozone concentration by measuring the absorption of light at specific wavelengths, offering high accuracy and reliability. Electrochemical analyzers rely on chemical reactions to detect ozone levels, providing a compact and cost-effective solution, though potentially less accurate. "Others" encompass various alternative technologies, such as portable devices and advanced sensor technologies, catering to diverse applications in air quality monitoring.</p></p>
