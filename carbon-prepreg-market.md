<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Carbon Prepreg Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoset</li><li>Thermoplastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon prepreg market is primarily divided into two types: thermoset and thermoplastic. Thermoset prepregs are made with resins that cure and harden irreversibly during the manufacturing process, providing high thermal and chemical resistance, making them ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. In contrast, thermoplastic prepregs utilize resins that can be re-melted and reshaped, offering advantages such as recyclability and faster processing times. Each type caters to specific performance requirements and applications across various industries, including construction and consumer goods.</p></p>
