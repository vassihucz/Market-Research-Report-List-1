<p><strong>The Domestic Smoke Alarms Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ionisation Type</li><li>Photoelectric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The domestic smoke alarms market is primarily categorized into two types: ionization and photoelectric. Ionization smoke alarms use radioactive material to detect fast-flaming fires, making them highly sensitive to small particles. In contrast, photoelectric smoke alarms utilize a light source and sensor to detect larger particles from slow-burning fires. Each type serves different safety needs, with ionization alarms being ideal for rapid flame detection and photoelectric alarms being more effective for smoldering fires, ensuring comprehensive home safety.</p></p>
