<p><ul><li>Heat Flux DSC</li><li>Power Compensated DSC</li><li>Modulated DSC</li><li>Hyper DSC</li><li>Pressure DSC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Differential Scanning Calorimetry (DSC) systems are categorized into several types based on their operational principles and applications. Heat Flux DSC measures temperature differences between a sample and a reference to determine heat flow. Power Compensated DSC maintains a constant temperature by adjusting power input to match sample heat flow. Modulated DSC adds a modulated temperature variation to enhance thermal analysis. Hyper DSC focuses on rapid scanning of high-temperature materials, while Pressure DSC examines the thermal properties of materials under varying pressures.</p></p>
