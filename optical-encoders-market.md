<p><strong>The Optical Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Encoders</li><li>Absolute Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical encoder market features two main types: incremental encoders and absolute encoders. Incremental encoders measure changes in position and provide relative feedback, ideal for applications needing directional movement tracking. In contrast, absolute encoders deliver precise position data at any moment, utilizing unique codes for each position, making them suitable for applications requiring accurate reference points. Both types serve diverse industries, including automation, robotics, and manufacturing, catering to varying needs for precision and application-specific functionalities.</p></p>
