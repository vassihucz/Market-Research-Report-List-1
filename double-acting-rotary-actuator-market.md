<p><p>The double-acting rotary actuator market includes various types, primarily balanced rotary actuators and rack-and-pinion pneumatic rotary actuators. Balanced rotary actuators offer efficient torque and load handling by employing a symmetrical design, minimizing wear and enhancing durability. Conversely, rack-and-pinion pneumatic rotary actuators convert linear motion from a rack into rotational motion of a pinion, providing strong torque output and precise control. Both types are utilized in diverse industrial applications, enhancing automation and process efficiency.</p></p>
