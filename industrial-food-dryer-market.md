<p><p>The industrial food dryer market comprises several types, each suited for specific drying needs. Spray dryers atomize liquid food into fine droplets, rapidly removing moisture. Freeze dryers preserve flavors and nutrients by sublimating ice under low temperatures. Fluidized-bed dryers use hot air to suspend and dry food particles evenly. Drum dryers apply heat to rotating drums for a continuous drying process. Vacuum dryers lower pressure to evaporate moisture efficiently, while tray dryers utilize heated trays for batch drying, catering to various food products.</p></p>
