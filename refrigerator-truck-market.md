<p><p>The refrigerator truck market comprises two main types: single temperature and multi-temperature trucks. Single temperature trucks are designed to maintain a consistent temperature throughout the cargo area, ideal for transporting perishable goods like fruits or dairy. In contrast, multi-temperature trucks feature separate compartments that can operate at different temperatures simultaneously, allowing for the transportation of diverse products, such as frozen items alongside fresh produce. Each type caters to specific logistical needs within the cold chain industry, ensuring optimal product preservation.</p></p>
