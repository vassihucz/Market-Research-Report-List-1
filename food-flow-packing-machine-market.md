<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food flow packing machines are categorized into horizontal and vertical markets based on their design and functionality. Horizontal flow packing machines operate by moving products horizontally through a series of rollers and sealing mechanisms, making them ideal for packaging various food items like snacks and baked goods. In contrast, vertical flow packing machines use a vertical arrangement to fill and seal products, suitable for powdered and granular items. Both types enhance efficiency and ensure product freshness while catering to different packaging needs.</p></p>
