<p><p>The annealed wafer market comprises primarily two types: Argon Annealed Wafers and Hydrogen Annealed Wafers. Argon Annealed Wafers are treated in an argon atmosphere, which helps prevent oxidation and maintain the purity of the silicon, enhancing electrical properties. In contrast, Hydrogen Annealed Wafers utilize hydrogen to improve crystallinity and remove impurities from the silicon surface, promoting better device performance. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, contributing to improved yield and efficiency in electronic applications.</p></p>
