Polypropylene clothing is lightweight and breathable, often used in medical and industrial applications for its durability. Polyethylene offers moisture resistance, making it suitable for environments requiring protection from liquids. Laminated polyesters combine the strengths of both materials with added barrier protection, ideal for handling hazardous substances. Each type caters to specific safety requirements across various industries, including healthcare, construction, and cleanrooms.</p></p>
