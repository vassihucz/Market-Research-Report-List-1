<p><p>Payroll outsourcing services can be categorized into two primary market types: Full-Managed Outsourcing and Co-Managed Outsourcing. Full-Managed Outsourcing involves a third-party provider taking complete control of all payroll functions, from processing to tax compliance, allowing businesses to focus on core operations. In contrast, Co-Managed Outsourcing offers a collaborative approach where the external provider and the internal HR team work together on payroll tasks, sharing responsibilities and insights, which enhances efficiency while retaining some in-house control.</p></p>
