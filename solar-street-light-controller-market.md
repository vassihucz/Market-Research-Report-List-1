<p><strong>The Solar Street Light Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MPPT Controller</li><li>PWM Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar street light controller market primarily consists of two types: MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking) controllers and PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) controllers. MPPT controllers optimize energy extraction from solar panels, adjusting voltage and current to maximize efficiency, making them suitable for higher energy demands and variable weather conditions. In contrast, PWM controllers regulate battery charging by switching the solar output on and off, making them simpler and cost-effective for lower power requirements. Both types cater to diverse applications in solar street lighting.</p></p>
