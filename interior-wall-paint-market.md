<p><p>The interior wall paint market encompasses three main types: water-based, oily, and dry powder paints. Water-based paints are popular for their quick drying time, low odor, and ease of cleaning, making them environmentally friendly. Oily paints, typically solvent-based, offer durable finishes but require longer drying times and produce stronger fumes. Dry powder paints, often used for industrial applications, provide a versatile solution with minimal waste, as they are mixed with a solvent just before application, ensuring a smooth finish.</p></p>
