<p><ul><li>Pretreatment</li><li>Cell Assembly</li><li>Post Processing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lithium battery manufacturing equipment encompasses three key market types: pretreatment, cell assembly, and post-processing. Pretreatment involves preparing materials, such as lithium and other metals, for battery production through processes like drying and mixing. Cell assembly focuses on assembling the individual components—anodes, cathodes, and electrolytes—into complete battery cells. Post-processing includes finishing operations like testing, inspection, and packaging to ensure batteries meet performance standards and quality requirements. Together, these stages are crucial for efficient and reliable lithium battery production.</p></p>
