<p><p>Q-Switched DPSS (Diode-Pumped Solid-State) lasers are categorized by their emission wavelengths, primarily 355nm, 532nm, and 1064nm, each serving distinct applications. The 355nm laser is ideal for precise micro-machining and UV curing, providing high energy at a short wavelength. The 532nm laser, known for its visible green light, is widely used in dermatology and aesthetic procedures. The 1064nm laser emits near-infrared light, suitable for deeper tissue applications and industrial uses. Additionally, "Others" encompass varying wavelengths or specialized configurations for niche applications.</p></p>
