<p><p>The film thickness measurement market is primarily composed of two key types: thickness monitors and spectrum ellipsometers. Thickness monitors utilize various techniques, such as quartz crystal microbalance or optical measurements, to provide real-time data on film thickness during deposition processes. Spectrum ellipsometers, on the other hand, employ light polarization to analyze material characteristics at a microscopic level, offering precise thickness measurements and optical properties of thin films. Both technologies are essential in industries like semiconductor manufacturing, optics, and coatings.</p></p>
