<p><p>The decontamination and infection equipment market is categorized into two main types: chamber decontamination and room decontamination. Chamber decontamination involves using specialized enclosed systems to sterilize medical instruments and small equipment, ensuring high-level disinfection. In contrast, room decontamination focuses on cleaning entire spaces, such as hospitals and clinics, using methods like fogging, UV light, or chemical sprays to eliminate pathogens from surfaces and the air, thereby enhancing overall safety and preventing infection transmission.</p></p>
