<p><p>The electric oil pump market is segmented into two primary types: integrated pumps and separate pumps. Integrated pumps are compact units that combine the motor and pump into a single assembly, offering space-saving advantages and streamlined installation, often used in vehicles and machinery. In contrast, separate pumps consist of distinct motor and pump components, allowing for greater flexibility in installation and maintenance. Each type serves different industry needs, catering to varying application requirements for efficiency and performance.</p></p>
