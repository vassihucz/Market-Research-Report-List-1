<p><ul><li>Passive Phased Array</li><li>Active Phased Array</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronically Scanned Array Radar (ESAR) technology is primarily categorized into two market types: Passive Phased Array and Active Phased Array. Passive Phased Array systems utilize fixed antennas with no transmitting capabilities, relying on external signals for operations. In contrast, Active Phased Array systems feature integrated transmitters and receivers, enabling dynamic beam steering and improved target tracking. Both technologies enhance radar performance, with Active Phased Array offering greater flexibility and adaptability, making it suitable for advanced military and commercial applications.</p></p>
