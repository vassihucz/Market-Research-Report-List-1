<p><ul><li>Hollow</li><li>Rigid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drive shaft market comprises mainly two types: hollow and rigid drive shafts. Hollow drive shafts are lightweight and offer better torque transmission due to their design, making them suitable for high-performance applications. They often enhance fuel efficiency and reduce overall vehicle weight. Rigid drive shafts, on the other hand, are more robust and provide better durability under extreme conditions. Despite being heavier, they can handle higher loads and are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles and industrial machinery.</p></p>
