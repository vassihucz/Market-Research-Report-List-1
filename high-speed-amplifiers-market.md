<p><p>The high-speed amplifiers market can be categorized into single power supply and dual power supply types. Single power supply amplifiers operate using one voltage source, making them simple and cost-effective for low-power applications. In contrast, dual power supply amplifiers utilize both positive and negative voltage sources, enhancing their performance in high-speed and high-frequency applications. This configuration allows for better dynamic range and linearity, making dual power supply amplifiers ideal for complex signal processing tasks in advanced technology sectors.</p></p>
