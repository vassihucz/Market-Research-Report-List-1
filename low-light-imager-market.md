<p><strong>The Low Light Imager Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low light imager market is primarily divided into two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) sensors. CCD sensors excel in capturing high-quality images with low noise, making them ideal for professional applications like astronomy and medical imaging. In contrast, CMOS sensors offer higher speeds, lower power consumption, and the ability to integrate additional circuitry, making them popular in consumer electronics like smartphones and security cameras. Both technologies continue to evolve, catering to varying performance and cost requirements.</p></p>
