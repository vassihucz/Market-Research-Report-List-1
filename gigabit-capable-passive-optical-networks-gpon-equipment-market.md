<p><strong>The Gigabit-capable Passive Optical Networks (GPON) Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FTTH/O</li><li>FTTB+LAN</li><li>FTTB+DSL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gigabit-capable Passive Optical Networks (GPON) equipment market can be segmented into three main types: FTTH/O, FTTB+LAN, and FTTB+DSL. FTTH/O (Fiber to the Home/Office) delivers high-speed internet directly to residential or business premises using fiber optics. FTTB+LAN (Fiber to the Building with Local Area Network) connects fiber to a building and distributes it using LAN technologies. FTTB+DSL (Fiber to the Building with Digital Subscriber Line) combines fiber and existing copper DSL lines, optimizing speed and reach for users.</p></p>
