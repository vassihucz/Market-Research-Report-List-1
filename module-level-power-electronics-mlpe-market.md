<p><p>Module Level Power Electronics (MLPE) encompasses technologies that enhance the efficiency and performance of solar photovoltaic systems at the module level. The primary types are power optimizers and microinverters. Power optimizers improve energy harvest from individual solar panels by maximizing their output, while microinverters convert the direct current generated by each panel into alternating current, allowing for greater flexibility and monitoring. Both technologies mitigate shading effects and provide enhanced energy yield, contributing to the growth of the solar energy market.</p></p>
