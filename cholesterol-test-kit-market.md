<p><p>The cholesterol test kit market is segmented into two primary types: fingerstick blood tests and venous blood tests. In contrast, venous blood tests require a sample drawn from a vein, typically conducted in a clinical setting for more comprehensive analysis. Both methods serve to assess cholesterol levels, aiding in cardiovascular health management.</p></p>
