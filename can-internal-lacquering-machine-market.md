<p><p>Internal lacquering machines are categorized based on the number of processing zones they have: three, six, and nine. A three-zone machine offers basic efficiency for smaller production needs, providing adequate coverage and drying. The six-zone variant enhances productivity, allowing for faster throughput and improved coating quality through additional drying and curing capabilities. The nine-zone machine is designed for high-volume operations, providing maximum drying efficiency and superior coating precision, suitable for large-scale manufacturing of coated containers or components.</p></p>
